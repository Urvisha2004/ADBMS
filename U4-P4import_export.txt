
Export the mca11 schema (excluding procedures manually):
exp mca11/mca11@orcl file=mca11_backup_np.dmp log=mca11_backup_np.log


Importing into the SCOTT Schema Using:
imp mca11/mca11@orcl FILE=mca11_backup_np.dmp LOG=mca11_backup_np.log FROMUSER=mca11 TOUSER=mca11

Loading Data from a Sequential File Using SQL*Loader:
sqlldr mca11@orcl control=control.ctl 
sqlldr mca11/mca11@orcl control=artist.ctl log=artist.log bad=artist.bad


Exporting Data from the SCOTT Schema:
exp mca11/mca11@orcl file=scott_export_np.dmp log=scott_export_np.log
